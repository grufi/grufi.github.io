What's Grufi?

Grufi is a web service designed for parents and teachers, which improves school-family communication.

Grufi allows to keep parents up to date in real time on projects carried out by the specialized staff of the school, through the reserved area, which allows access to all the multimedia material, by mobile or any other device: PC, tablet or smartphone.

Grufi, allows parents to create moments with their children, where they become the protagonists, through photos, videos, documents. Children can tell what happened during their day, the important discoveries and experiences made, pearls of life at school.

Grufi, allows teachers using a computer, to share children's activities and projects with their families. Grufi reduces some work for teachers, such as compilation of daily activities, therefore, saving time to be used with students.
